# Novella

**Learn languages through engaging stories.**

Novella is an innovative language learning app that helps you master new languages through immersive, AI-generated stories tailored to your level.

## Features

- **Story-based Learning**: Learn vocabulary and grammar through context
- **Multiple Languages**: English, Turkish, Spanish, German, Japanese, Korean, and more
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Stories matched to your CEFR level (A1-B2)
- **Spaced Repetition**: Smart review system to help you remember
- **Audio Support**: Listen and improve your pronunciation
